Our Guarantee

MATERIAL

All our raw material is sourced and cut by ourselves throughout the lands of Ireland. Our countryside is full of woodlands, mountains, rivers and streams- ideal locations for the trees and bushes to thrive and grow.

SEASONING

After cutting our sticks, not much is done to them for a minimum of 12 months. Depending on the wood they can sometime be left to season for two years. Our sticks are cut normally during October to Late February.

DESIGN

Our designs are more for working stick rather than a show stick. We take pride that our sticks are light, strong and like an extra arm to whoever is using it.

FINISH

A stick needs to be finished with the best quality of varnish or stain. All ours are carefully prepared before apply the varnish. They are left to dry before a second coat is applied.
