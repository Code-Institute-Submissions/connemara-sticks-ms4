Features

Material

Our wooden walking sticks are handcrafted in Ireland from the highest quality native trees. Crafted from beautiful and durable materials, we cut all our raw material during the winter months and are seasoned for a minimum of 12 months.

Made In Ireland

We offer an unbeatable selection of handmade wooden walking sticks for hiking. Our hiking sticks are beautifully crafted by ourselves in our workshop where we guarantee no two sticks are the same..

Customization

We offer a wide range of accessories, including compasses, badges and Connemara marble. Have an idea or are looking to have one made for a special person, contact us with your idea and we will happily help try bring that idea alive.
